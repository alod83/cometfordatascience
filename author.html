Angelica Lo Duca

Angelica Lo Duca is a researcher at the Institute of Informatics and Telematics of the National Research Council, Italy.

She is also an external professor of Data Journalism at the University of Pisa.

Her research interests include Data Science, Data Journalism, and Web Applications. She used to work on Network Security, Semantic Web, Linked Data, and Blockchain.

She has published more than 40 scientific papers at national and international conferences and journals. She has participated in different national and international projects, and events. She has been a member of the Program Committee at different conferences. She is also part of the Editorial Team of the HighTech And Innovation Journal. She owns a personal blog, where she publishes articles on her research interests.
